1

Call Of Duty: Advanced Warfare

Description: Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare, developed by Sledgehammer Games (co-developers of Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 3), harnesses the first three-year, all next-gen development cycle in franchise history. Call of Duty: Advanced Warfare envisions the powerful battlegrounds of the future, where both technology and tactic have evolved to usher in a new era of combat for the franchise. Delivering a stunning performance, Academy Award winning actor Kevin Spacey stars as Jonathan Irons - one of the most powerful men in the world - shaping this chilling vision of the future of war.

Review: Call of Duty: Advanced Wafare is the newest Call of Duty game. With new features and weapons, such as grappling hook and jetpacks, Advanced Warfare was my top favorite game. The campaign and multiplayer are awesome.

2

WWE 2k15

Description WWE 2K15 will deliver the most authentic recreation of WWE action to date, featuring your favorite WWE Superstars, Divas, and Legends as seen on WWE television each week. With new game modes such as Who Got NXT? and 2K Showcase, in addition to returning modes like WWE Universe, a comprehensive Creation Suite, and all your favorite match types, WWE 2K15 promises to put all of WWE in your hands.

Review: is the most anticipated WWE game of the year. But there was some dissapointements. 2K takes aways some of the old features and creations. Many people are looking forward to playing 'My Career' mode on next-gen consoles.

****

3

Destiny

Description: Everything changed with the arrival of the Traveler. It sparked a Golden Age when our civilization spanned our solar system, but it didn't last. Something hit us, knocked us down. The survivors built a city beneath the Traveler, and have begun to explore our old worlds, only to find them filled with deadly foes. You are a Guardian of the last safe city on Earth, able to wield incredible power. Defend the City. Defeat our enemies. Reclaim all that we have lost. Be brave.

Review: From the creators of Halo, Destiny is a futuristic game that is addicting. One thing that was a downgrade was that it's an online-only game.

****
