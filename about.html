About

GameRadar is a game review website informing you on the top games out today. Game radar was created by Justin Wright with the help of Will Vedder. Justin is a student at John Barry Middle School and Will a developer at Bluecadet Interactive.Justin thought of the idea of making a game review website informing people on his top 5 games. The site was incorporated a wireframing process for 3 templates and developed using HTML and CSS. This project followed a schedule allowing for flexibility.

Game radar was developed using HTML/CSS, a common computer code for creating websites. We thought of reviews, descriptions, and designs. We planned project directions, decided to develop game review website informing viewers on the top 5 games out today such as WWE 2k15, Call of Duty Advanced Warfare, and Grand Theft Auto 5 (now available for Playstation 4 and Xbox One). It took weeks to think of the perfect designs and reviews and even the top 5 games to choose from.

The love of gaming was the ultimate inspiration behind GameRadar.
